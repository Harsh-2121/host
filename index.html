Our Story

For over 20 years, our kitchen has been serving the most basic yet most cherished foods - fresh paneer and soft roti, made exactly the way our grandmothers taught us.

Started by Mrs. Dhana with a simple mission: to provide pure, affordable, and traditional food to our community. Every batch of paneer is freshly made each morning, and every roti is hand-rolled with care.

No preservatives. No shortcuts. Just honest, traditional food made with love.
